Competition Formats
01
Four Balls
Best Ball
Two players per team each play their own ball throughout the hole. The lower score of the pair counts for the team. The side with the lower best-ball score wins the hole.
02
Scramble
Texas Scramble
Both players tee off, the best shot is selected, and both play from that spot. This continues until the ball is holed. Favours teamwork and consistent ball striking.
03
Alternate Shot
Foursomes
Partners share a single ball, alternating shots throughout the hole. One player tees off on odd holes, the other on even. Demands precise communication and course management.
04
Singles
Match Play · Final
One-on-one match play. All 16 players face off in head-to-head singles. The decisive session that determines the Season 3 champion team.
